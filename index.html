Q: Is there any way to reserve court time without booking a cabana party?
A: No. Aside from cabana packages, courts are rented strictly on a first come, first served basis.

Q: For cabana parties, is it required that all of my guests purchase the same drink package? If so, what about my non-drinking and or pregnant friends?
A: Yes. All members attending your party, even if they're not drinking or playing, must purchase the same bar package. However, non-alcoholic beverages are included in all of our packages. No exceptions will be made.

Q: What happens if our final guest count ends up being more or less than our original contract?
A: You have until 72 hours before your event to adjust your headcount up or down within 4 people. (All reservations have a 10 person minimum). Please note that after contract has been signed only one change in headcount can be accommodated. Last minute additional guests will be added to your bill for the full 2 hour event, regardless of how long he or she is there. Please note, raising your headcount may not result in an additional court/cabana and service may suffer as a result of overcrowding. All additional last minute guests will be accommodated at management's discretion.

Q: Do I need to provide a guest list?
A: Yes. You have up until 48 hours before your event to provide a final headcount and guest list. Your listed guests must show up 30 minutes before your event's start time for VIP entry. If there's a line, anyone not on the guest list will have to wait for admittance.

Q: I see that Monday and Tuesday are league nights. Can I still book a cabana party?
A: We cannot book cabana parties for those nights as all courts are used for league play.

Q: If I book a cabana party, what happens when our two hours are up? Do we have to leave or can we stay and pay for extra time / bar service?
A: Once your party is over you'll be asked to vacate your cabana/court but you're definitely welcome to continue hanging out and enjoying all the tropical fun that The Royal Palms has to offer.
